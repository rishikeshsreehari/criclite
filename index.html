*** PROJECT CURRENTLY ON HOLD ***

Due to issues with finding a reliable and affordable live cricket score provider that aligns with our open-source project goals, CricLite is currently on hold.

We're actively working to find a sustainable solution for providing live cricket data. The challenge is finding a provider that offers:

  • Reliable live cricket scores
  • Affordable pricing for open-source projects
  • Comprehensive coverage of international matches
  • API stability and good documentation

We apologize for the inconvenience. If you know of any good solutions or are aware of cricket data providers that might work for our project, please reach out to us!

Know a solution? Contact us:
Email: hello@rishikeshs.com
GitHub: https://github.com/rishikeshsreehari/criclite/issues
