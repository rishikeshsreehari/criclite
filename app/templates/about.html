About CricLite

CricLite is a minimalist cricket score website designed for quick access to live match information, with a focus on simplicity and performance.

Features

  • Plain text interface - works on any device or browser
  • Fast loading - minimal overhead and no bulky JavaScript
  • Terminal friendly - easily view scores in your command line
  • Live updates - scores refresh automatically
  • Dark and light themes

Why Plain Text?

CricLite embraces simplicity. The plain text format loads quickly on slow connections and displays consistently across all devices. It's also perfect for viewing in terminal windows or text-only browsers.

Terminal Access

Get live scores directly in your terminal:

Linux/Mac: curl -s https://criclite.com/plain.txt
Windows: curl.exe -s https://criclite.com/plain.txt
